Biography of Eric

Who is Eric?

Ever since I was a young child, I knew I needed to be in the IT field one way or another. My foot in the door moment was my best friends shop where he built custom computers and installed car neon lights in the cases before RGB. From there I went to school and majored in CS and dabbled in my share engineering. I left to work my way through some amazing companies as a Network/System Engineer. There was always a side of me that was not happy though. I needed more, I wanted to learn it all. It started by using PowerShell to automate simple tasks that were too mundane. I moved on to Linux enviroments and Bash. But it just did not have what I wanted. It had its limitations. So that is when I moved to Python and JavaScript. I was able to do so much with Pyhton, and make my websites amazing with JavaScript.

Be a Developer

2. Document everything.

4. Read the documentation/manual.

5. Save all emails.

6. Research as much as possible.

7. Love your job.
